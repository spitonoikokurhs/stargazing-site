About me

Born and raised on Kos Island, I design and host Stargazing experiences across luxury hotels, resorts and private events.

Now entering its 5th year, the experience has been developed and presented in multiple 5-star properties, combining astronomy, storytelling and atmosphere into a refined guest experience.

My goal is to help guests connect with the night sky in a way that feels simple, engaging and memorable.

Base: Kos Island, Greece
Coverage: Nearby islands, Greece, Bodrum
Focus: Guest experience, storytelling, astronomy, uranography
Style: Premium, calm, immersive
