About me

Hi, I’m Michalis Reisis — a local Greek, born and raised on Kos Island. I’m an amateur astronomer and advanced astrophotographer, hosting stargazing events for the past four years on Kos, in other areas of Greece and in nearby Bodrum.

I love to travel and create high‑quality images that people remember. My goal is to help guests explore the night sky in a way that feels personal, elegant and memorable.
