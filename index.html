About me

Born and raised on Kos Island, I am an amateur astronomer and advanced astrophotographer creating premium stargazing experiences across Kos, nearby islands, Greece, and Bodrum.

For the past four years I have hosted astronomy nights for hotels, guests and private events, combining observation through telescopes with storytelling, education and atmosphere.

I love to travel and create images that people remember. My goal is to help guests explore the night sky in a way that feels personal, elegant and memorable.

Base: Kos Island, Greece
